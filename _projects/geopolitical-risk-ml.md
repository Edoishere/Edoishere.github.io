Research project in collaboration with the **University of Turin**, **Ministry of Foreign Affairs**, and **Intesa San Paolo**.

### Highlights
- Neural network model for geopolitical risk forecasting  
- Interactive visualization for risk exploration  
- Application of gradient-based explainability methods  

### Technologies
Python, PyTorch, Grad-CAM, Plotly, Dash
